### Third-party Assets
AudioFFT utilizes the following third-party assets:

---
**Qt 6**
*   Usage: Graphical User Interface (GUI)
*   License: GNU LGPL version 3

---
**FFmpeg**
*   Usage: Audio file reading and decoding
*   License: GNU LGPL version 2.1 or later

---
**FFTW3**
*   Usage: Fast Fourier Transform (FFT)
*   License: GNU GPL version 2 or later

---
**libjpeg-turbo**
*   Usage: JPEG image encoding
*   License: IJG

---
**libpng**
*   Usage: PNG image encoding
*   License: libpng/zlib

---
**zlib**
*   Usage: Data compression (dependency of libpng)
*   License: zlib

---
**libtiff**
*   Usage: TIFF image encoding
*   License: BSD-style

---
**OpenJPEG**

**libwebp**

**libavif**
